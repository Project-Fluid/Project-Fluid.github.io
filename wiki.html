Guidelines

If you are willing to maintain fluid for your device, take these into consideration:

- YOU MUST OWN THE DEVICE and be able to test yourself in case of emergency
- You must have proper git knowledge (cherry picking, fixing conflicts, squashing etc.)
- Some basic knowledge of reading logcats and managing most device releated errors/bugs
- Any modifications to the rom source should be approved by the dev team
- Major source changes can’t happen
- The maintainer must ship builds monthly
- The maintainer must build with the OFFICIAL build tag (lmao)
- The maintainer must speak english good enough to be understandable
- Device specific groups are not required
- XDA threads are recommended but not required

Device requirements

Your builds must fulfill these in order to get official status:

- Enforcing builds are recommended but not mandatory
- Most phone functionality should work
- The device kernel should be public
- While prebuilt kernels are allowed, they are not recommended
- An unofficial build has to be done before applying for official

If you fulfill all of this, what are you waiting for?
