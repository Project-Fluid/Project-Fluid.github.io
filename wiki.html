About us

Hello there!

Let us introduce ourselves,

Fluid is a project based on Android Open Source Project (AOSP), aiming at a cleaner and fluidic UI with stability at core with themes and customisation. You could proudly say this ain’t some random custom rom with cherry-picked customisation :p
Take your time and enjoy fluid, hope you like it!

The Team

Guidelines

If you are willing to maintain fluid for your device, take these into consideration:

- YOU MUST OWN THE DEVICE and be able to test yourself in case of emergency
- You must have proper git knowledge (cherry picking, fixing conflicts, squashing etc.)
- Some basic knowledge of reading logcats and managing most basic device releated errors/bugs
- Any modifications to the rom source should be approved by the dev team
- Major source changes by the maintainer can’t happen
- The maintainer must ship builds monthly
- The maintainer must build with the OFFICIAL build tag
- The maintainer must speak english good enough to be understandable
- Device specific groups are not required
- XDA threads are not required

Device requirements

Your builds must fulfill these in order to get official status:

- Enforcing builds are recommended but not mandatory
- Most phone functionality should work
- The device kernel MUST be public (when not prebuilt)
- While prebuilt kernels are allowed, DO NOT ship a prebuilt kernel if you have working kernel sources availble
- An unofficial build has to be done before applying for official

If you fulfill all of this, what are you waiting for?
